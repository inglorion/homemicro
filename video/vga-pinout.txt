Legend:
-:  No pin, or unconnected
.:  Unconnected pin
0:  Ground
B+: Blue signal
B0: Blue ground
G+: Green signal
G0: Green ground
H:  Hsync signal
R+: Red signal
R0: Red ground
S0: Sync ground
V:  Vsync signal
